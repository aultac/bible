

**Summary: Genesis 9-11**

**Title:** **God gives his rainbow covenant to never flood the earth again as the earth is repopulated and dispersed from Babel, showing all nations came from Noah but Ham’s descendants through Canaan are cursed.**

**Storyline Summary:**

- **Noah sacrifices the clean animals**
- **God is pleased: anthropomorphic terms**
- **We can eat meat now.**
- **Do not murder: murderers must be put to death**
- **God’s covenant not to destroy the earth again**
- **Geneaologies of the 70 nations**
- **Babel: nations are divided**
- **Geneaology of Abram**
