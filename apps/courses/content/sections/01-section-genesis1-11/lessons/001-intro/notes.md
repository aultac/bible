# 001-Intro

- Get everyone on slack: sign up sheet
- We believe this book not because we grew up here, not because it’s our culture, not because we want to, but because it is true. There is an objective truth out there. Truth is that which corresponds to reality, it exists whether you believe it or not. It is not an "average" of all the beliefs out there.
- This will take effort. If you say “I’m not good at reading.” Go home, look in the mirror, pray “God, please help me to get your word into my head.” Then open your eyes tell yourself “Stop making excuses.” Man up. Things that you do not sacrifice for, you do not value. It is the work that creates the value.
- Visual reading is NOT the preferred method: listening is. Audio bible. Verse by verse commentary.
- Repetition is the key to memory. This should start to feel like “I’ve already heard this a million times”
- You must bring your intellect to the Bible: those who think that you shut it off and you read about religion, have not studied the Bible seriously enough. If I write this on the board "We Have Reason to Believe", do you agree? (point out second meaning)
- You CAN understand this! It was written for you. Pray. Use Slack!
- How to read the Bible: obvious informs non-obvious. Psalm 14
