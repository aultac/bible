

**Summary: Genesis 17-19**

**Title:** **God re-affirms Abrahamic covenant through changing Abraham and Sarah’s name before Abraham argues with God over destroying the righteous with the wicked in Sodom, after which Lot escapes to a cave with his daughters who bare his children as the fathers of the Moabite and Ammonite peoples.**

**Storyline Summary:**

- **Abram’s name changed to Abraham**
- **God confirms covenant (have a son) with Abraham**
- **Sarah’s name changed**
- **Circumcision**
- **Abraham argues for Sodom**
- **Sodom and Gomorrah are destroyed**
- **Lot and his daughters in the cave have a “relationship”**
