

**Summary: Genesis 36-37:28**

**Title:** **The descendants of Esau (Edom) and Joseph’s dreams get him thrown into a pit by his brothers and sold into slavery.**

**Storyline Summary:**

- **Descendants of Esau**
- **Amelekites harass Israel and God wipes them out eventually**
- **Jesus is persecuted by the eventual descendants of Esau: Herod’s**
- **Esau was the father of the “Idumeans” (Herod’s were Idumean)**
- **“Haman the Agagite” in Ester descended from Amelekite king Agag**
- **Joseph has a dream**
- **Joseph’s brothers hate him**
- **Joseph was the favorite**
- **Joseph’s brothers threw him in a well**
- **“They” pulled him out and sold him**
