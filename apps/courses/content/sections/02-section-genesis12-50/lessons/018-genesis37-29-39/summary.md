

**Summary: Genesis 37:29-39**

**Title:** **Jacob identifies Joseph’s blood stained robe, Tamar deceives Judah to get herself a child after Judah fails to give her his final son. Joseph is unjustly accused by Potiphar’s wife and put in jail.**

**Storyline Summary:**

- **Rueben and brothers return and the goat blood trick with Joseph’s robe**
- **Jacob mourns**
- **Midianite traders traded Joseph to Potiphar**
- **Judah leaves the family for Chezib**
- **Judah marries a Canaanite and has kids**
- **Two of his sons killed for being evil**
- **Tamar tricks Judah and has Perez and Zerah**
- **Joseph takes over Potiphar’s household**
- **Potiphar’s wife accuses Joseph**
- **Joseph thrown in prison**
