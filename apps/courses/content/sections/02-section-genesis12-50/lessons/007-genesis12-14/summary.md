

**Summary: Genesis 12-14**

**Title:** **God calls out Abram through Abrahamic Covenant before Abram goes to Egypt, after which he has to save Lot in the slaughter of the kings resulting in Melchizedek’s blessing.**

**Storyline Summary:**

- God promises a nation to Abram
- Abram and Sarai go to Egypt because of famine
- God afflicted Pharoah and he sent Abram back with wealth
- Abram and Lot go their separate ways on their return
- Abram rescues Lot
- Melchizedek blesses Abram
