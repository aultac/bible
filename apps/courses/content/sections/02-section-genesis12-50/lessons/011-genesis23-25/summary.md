

**Summary: Genesis 22-25**

**Title:** **Sarah and Abraham die and Isaac gets a wife and sons.**

**Storyline Summary:**

- **Sarah dies and Abraham purchases her tomb in Canaan**
- **Abraham sends his servant to find a wife in his homeland for Isaac**
- **God leads him to Rebekah and she returns and marries Isaac**
- **Abraham marries Keturah and births 6 children**
- **Abraham dies and is buried with Sarah, Ishmael has 12 sons**
- **Ishmael dies and Ishmael descendants are hostile toward all of their relatives**
- **Rebekah has twins, Esau and Jacob**
- **Esau the hunter sells his birthright to Jacob who Rebekah loves.**
