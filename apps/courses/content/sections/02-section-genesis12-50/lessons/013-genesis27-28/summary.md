

**Summary: Genesis 27:18-28**

**Title:** **Jacob’s deception gets him Esau’s blessing but forces him to Laban through Bethel.**

**Storyline Summary:**

- **Jacob deceives his father at the behest of his mother**
- **Isaac blesses Jacob**
- **Esau is very distraught upon discovery of the deception**
- **Jacob flees to Paddan-aram**
- **Jacob has a dream of Jacob’s ladder**
- **Jacob names “Bethel” “house of God”**
- **Jacob makes a vow to God**
