

**Summary: Genesis 26-27:18**

**Title:** **Isaac deals with Philistines, digs wells, is comforted by God’s direct promise, and is deceived by Rebekah and Jacob: dealing, digging, directed, and deceived.**

- **Isaac deceives Abimelech again with the sister trick**
- **God confirms his covenant to Isaac**
- **Isaac starts digging wells**
- **Isaac becomes prosperous enough to get kicked out, then enough to be courted back in**
- **Isaac covenant with Abimelech at Beer’sheba**
- **Rebekah’s big deception to get the blessing for Jacob**
- **Esau marries Hittite women**
