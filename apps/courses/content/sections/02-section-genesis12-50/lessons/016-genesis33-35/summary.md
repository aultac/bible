

**Summary: Genesis 33-35**

**Title:** **Esau is nice but Jacob moves on: Dinah defiled, Shechem slaughtered, Bethel blessing, Rachel RIP**

**Storyline Summary:**

- **Jacob meets Esau**
- **Jacob and Esau “reconcile”**
- **Jacob leaves, Esau goes home**
- **Jacob bought near Shechem**
- **Jacob builds an altar at Shechem**
- **Rape of Dinah by Shechem**
- **Dinah’s brother’s outraged**
- **All the men of Shechem circumcised**
- **Simeon and Levi slaughter the whole town**
- **Fear of God fell on all surrounding nations**
- **Jacob returns to Bethel and is blessed and builds an altar**
- **Rachel gave birth to Benjamin**
- **Rachel dies and is buried on the side of the road from Bethel to Bethlehem**
- **Jacob makes it back home.**
- **Isaac dies and Esau and Jacob bury him.**
