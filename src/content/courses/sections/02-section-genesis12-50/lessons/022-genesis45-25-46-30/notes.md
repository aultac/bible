  - GOOD NEWS: It doesn’t matter what your opinion is on the question. Regardless of whether things are determined, or we have free will, our understanding of the fact does not change it. **You and I will not be on the throne at judgment day, deciding. **Genesis 45:25-end > Jacob learns Joseph is alive**

- HEART BECAME NUMB: what a powerful description of what Jacob felt. Every time the boys leave, they come home with life-changing stories.
- DID THE BROTHERS TELL THE TRUTH? The story does not say. Jacob could still be under the impression that the brothers just found Joseph’s coat on the way back from Shechem and Dothan. The new story could be that he must have just lost his coat when he got kidnapped by Midianites and sold into slavery. **We don’t know if Jacob ever found out what really happened.** I don’t think he ever did because **he doesn’t mention it at all in the blessings/curses** to the boys at the end of his life.
- JACOB’S AWFUL LIFE ENDS WELL: his son was dead for 20 years, now he’s moving to Egypt with all the grandkids to be with Joseph. Deceiving Isaac, Esau, Laban, Leah and Rachel and their fight for children and affection, wrestling with God, Dinah’s rape, Shechem’s slaughter, Bilhah’s defilement by Reuben, awful dad to 10 of 12 sons, loss of Joseph, famine, having to let Benjamin go to Egypt. **Now he will die among all his sons with wealth, at peace, and full of years.**

**Genesis 46:1-4 > God tells Jacob Egypt is Ok**

- HE WAS ALREADY GOING: so why does God appear to him? **I think he was planning to go and come home.** God’s appearance is what tells him he’s going to stay in Egypt. “Do not be afraid” is what God tells humanity the most. Jacob may have been afraid to go, perhaps at 130 he thought he would not survive the journey. So it makes sense to God ask God first, especially after he became Israel and turned his life to God.
- ECHOED OF ORIGINAL MESSAGE ON FLIGHT FROM ESAU AT JACOB’S LADDER IN BETHEL: **Read Genesis 28:13-15**
- GODS DID NOT TRAVEL TO OTHER GOD’S TERRITORIES: this was unique to Jacob‘s God. The gods were tied to the dirt of their territory. Read Moses, standing on holy ground, Naaman carrying dirt back. The other gods needed humans to carry their idols when they traveled to give them a conduit into space that was not their own. Jacob’s God was completely unique: he could go anywhere he wanted because he’s the one that set up the whole territorial system in the first place when he gave them their inheritance of the other nations at Babel. **Jacob’s God carried him instead of Jacob carrying his God’s idol.**
- JOSEPH’S HAND SHALL CLOSE YOUR EYES: nothing in Jacob’s life has been as poetically moving as the peaceful idea that the hand of Joseph shall close his eyes. One of the most moving lines in all literature. How many years must he have cried and prayed, if he could just see Joseph again?
- HENRY BAMFORD PARKES LINEAR HISTORY: in “The Divine Order”, published in 1969, had the thesis that all other cultures viewed history as cyclical. A spinning wheel forever in the same place. Seasons repeat, kings rise and fall, floods and golden ages come and go. They were incapable of even conceiving of “progress”, because history is just a circle. From page 10: “**Judaism \[starting with the Torah\] repudiated the cyclic view of history held by all other ancient peoples and affirmed that it was a meaningful process leading to the gradual regeneration of humanity. This was the origin of the Western belief in progress.**” Looks at Hindus and Buddhists today with the concept of Karma and the cycle of Samsara, for example. Even the gods are subject to the cycle. But the Torah was completely different. History is linear in the Torah. “Progress” is able to be defined, and after defined, it is possible.
- WESTERN CIVILIZATION IS BASED ON LINEAR HISTORY: this is one reason the West was different than all other civilizations. It’s Christian roots allowed it to have “progress” its core. That we can work to make things better. That we can discover, and build, and stand on the shoulders of the intellectual giants that came before us. We don’t just live, love, die, repeat, with no purpose or meaning. This is only possible if you have a linear view of history.
- LONG-TERM PROPHECY REQUIRES LINEAR HISTORY: The introduction of long-term prophecy from the Torah, God promising land to Israel after slavery in Egypt, promising to one day bless all the nations, promising Jacob that it’s ok to go to Egypt because God has a plan, this is all foundational to the worldview of linear history that is unique to the Torah and by extension Christianity. **It’s an implicit bias we have growing up in Western civilization.**

**Genesis 46:26-27 > 70 people (or 75?)**

- 66 COUNTING UP TO 70: Jacob and Joseph get in there with 2 sons. Plus the wives is probably about a hundred at least of just family. Plus servants and servant’s families.
- DISCREPANCY WITH SEPTUAGINT: Septuagint lists 5 more grandsons of Joseph and says 75 people. Steven, in Acts, quotes Septuagint as 75. Our 70 is based on Masoretic text. Septuagint is 1200 years older. But Masoretic Hebrew text says 70 and doesn’t list Joseph’s grandsons, so ESV went with 70. Also aligns with Dueteronomy 10:22 is 70 in all translations. But Exodus 1:5 is 75 in non-Masoretic’s as well. Seems likely that the longer list is more accurate and it got shortened at some point to align with Deuteronomy 10:22. But that verse could easily be read as a ballpark figure in Deuteronomy. Meaning you went to Egypt with very few people, and you left with a whole lot. If I had to bet, the original said 75, but none of modern English translators agree with me, they all use the Maseretic text.
- WHERE OUR BIBLE CAME FROM: —> **MAPS FOR EMPIRES!!!**
  - IMPORTANT: why care about translation history? 2 people:
    - Charles Tayes Russel: founder of Jehovah’s Witnesses, started by claiming that all Bible translations were wrong. Published his own “Studies in the Scriptures” and told people to read that instead of the Bible. Now, generations of Jehovah’s Witnesses live in quiet sadness—trapped in fear of the organization’s disfellowshipping and cut off from the true freedom and joy found in Jesus Christ.
    - Joseph Smith: founder of Mormonism, claimed to have been visited by the angel “Moroni” who told him all the Christian denominations were wrong because all their translations were wrong. And Joseph was to lead humanity back to the one true church. Joseph Smith later made his own translation, in which he added 13 verses to the end of Genesis 50 after Joseph’s death prophesying that one day another Joseph, son of Joseph, would arise to restore the true church. He wrote himself in to the Bible and called it a “translation”.
  - 250BC: SEPTUAGINT (means “70”): Koine Greek translation of Hebrew, spread after Alexander the Great
  - 250BC-70AD: DEAD SEA SCROLLS (first discovered in 1947): Caves at Qumran where Essenes lived who copied texts for a living and sold them. Proof that Old Testament prophecies of the Messiah were not written after his birth. Proof that Masoretic text today is miraculously consistent with the text over 2000 years ago.
  - 405AD: LATIN VULGATE: translation by Jerome (“vulgar” meaning “for the common man”) for “Catholic” (as in “proper”) church for about 1100 years.
  - 1000AD: MASORETIC: Jewish scribal sect religiously devoted to perfect transmission of the text, including lines and hand strokes, added the vowels and punctuation. This became the “standard” Hebrew Old Testament.
  - 1054AD: GREAT SCHISM: Constantinople refused to be controlled by Rome. Constantinople wanted a “first among equals” status with Rome, and Rome refused. Church split into East and West. **East kept Septuagint, West kept Vulgate.**
  - 1382AD: WYCLIFFE ENGLISH BIBLE: John Wycliffe translates Latin Vulgate into English. Handwritten, no printing press yet. He is rebuked by Catholic Church as heretic, but not arrested or executed.
  - 1440AD: PRINTING PRESS: Gutenburg Bible (Latin Vulgate) is first thing published.
  - 1516: ERASMUS: (Novum Instrumentum omne: “Complete New Testament”) Erasmus publishes first-ever full Greek New Testament from collected Greek manuscripts.
  - 1517: Protestant Reformation: Martin Luther breaks from Rome. Driven by “Sola Scriptura”, reformers emphasized returning to original Hebrew and Greek. Masoretic for Old Testament and Erasmus’s text for new.
  - 1526: Tyndale: William Tyndale published first “printed” English New Testament translated directly from Greek (building on Wycliff Bible that was English translated from the Latin Vulgate). Catholic Church arrests and **executes him in 1536 before he can finish**. He wanted the common man to be able to read the Bible. The Catholic Church said if the common man reads the Bible without us watching over his shoulder, he will reach wrong conclusions. So they killed Tyndale. Far cry from the Vulgate days. This is not the position of the Catholic Church today. But the idea that someone has to watch over your shoulder to make sure you interpret how they want you to interpret is exactly how the Watchtower keeps Jehovah’s Witnesses enslaved to their organization today going back to their founder, Charles Taze Russel.
  - 1535: Coverdale Bible: when they arrested Tyndale, Myles Coverdale used his New Testament and Torah and finished it out to the first full English translation of the Bible. Killing Tyndale failed to stop God’s word getting out.
  - 1611: King James Version: commissioned by…King James I for a unifying English bible apart from Catholic Church that England had broken from 77 years before. 80% from Tyndale.
