

**Summary: Genesis 31-32**

**Title:** **Jacob and family flee Laban with God's help only for him to wrestle with God, emerging as a limping Israel, as Esau takes up the chase.**

**Storyline Summary:**

- **Jacob flees from Laban in secret**
- **Laban finds out and catches up to him**
- **Rachel stole Laban’s household gods**
- **God told Laban not to say anything good or bad to Jacob**
- **Laban searches for the gods**
- **Rachel lies about the gods and Laban doesn’t find them**
- **Jacob gets a backbone and argues with Laban**
- **Covenant to not cross the line, part ways**
- **Jacob divides his camp in 2 in case Esau attacks**
- **Jacob sends gifts to placate Esau**
- **Jacob wrestles with God in fear of his brother Esau**
- **Jacob gets a new name, Israel, as “struggle with God”**
