

**Summary: Genesis 29-30**

**Title:** **Laban deceives Jacob for his wives and livestock while Rachel and Leah compete over love and kids.**

**Storyline Summary:**

- **Jacob meets Rachel at the well**
- **Jacob kisses Rachel: Bedazzled**
- **Laban deceives Jacob with Leah**
- **Jacob works 7 more years for Rachel**
- **Leah gave birth to sons, Rachel is jealous. Leah was ok with that.**
- **Leah’s fourth son is Judah meaning “praise” -> Judah leads to Jesus**
- **Rachel gives Bilhah to Jacob and gets a surrogate son**
- **Leah gives Zilpah**
- **Mandrakes and more sons**
- **Rachel has Joseph**
- **Jacob gets rich on the spotted goat market**
