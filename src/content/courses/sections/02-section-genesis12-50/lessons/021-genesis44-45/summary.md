

**Summary: Genesis 44-45:24**

**Title:** **Joseph brothers are tested and it is found that Benjamin has the silver cup, Joseph reveals who he is, and pharaoh sends them back to get their families.**

**Storyline Summary:**

- **Jimmy Steward put the cup in Benjamin’s sack**
- **Brother’s leave, steward catches up**
- **Brothers deny having the cup**
- **Judah speaks for the brothers, promises slavery and death if they have the cup**
- **Find cup in Benjamin’s sack**
- **Back to Egypt with heads hung low**
- **Joseph confronts them**
- **Judah has his big self-sacrificial plea moment**
- **JOSEPH IS REVEALED!**
- **Joseph says it was God’s plan, not their fault he’s in Egypt**
- **Go home and get the family!**
