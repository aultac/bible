

**Summary: Genesis 40-41:45**

**Title:** **Joseph interprets dreams in prison, cup bearer forgets and remembers Joseph, leading Pharaoh to put Joseph in charge of all of Egypt.**

**Storyline Summary:**

- **Aaron made his first mistake: 2 birthdays in the Bible**
- **Cupbearer and baker have dreams**
- **Joseph interprets them**
- **Cupbearer is pardoned, baker is hanged**
- **Cupbearer forgot about Joseph for 2 years**
- **Pharoah has a dream**
- **Pharoah’s magicians could not interpret it**
- **Cupbearer remembers Joseph**
- **Joseph is put in charge of all Egypt, second only to Pharoah**
- **Joseph is married to Asenath priest of On’s daughter.**
