

**Summary: Genesis 41:45-43**

**Title:** **Joseph prepared Egypt for famine, attracting his brothers to buy food and Joseph tests them to get Benjamin to Egypt.**

**Storyline Summary:**

- **Dream interpretation turned out to be true**
- **2 sons born to Joseph and Asenath: Ephraim and Manasseh**
- **Plenty and then Famine**
- **Brothers head to Egypt without Benjamin**
- **Joseph accuses them of being spies**
- **Joseph has some fun with his brothers to test them**
- **Simeon in prison until Benjamin comes back**
- **Money was back in the bags with the grain when they got home**
- **Hunger convinced Jacob finally to send Benjamin**
- **They took back the silver as honest men.**
- **“Dinner party of Cain” with Joseph**
