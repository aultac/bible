

**Summary: Genesis 20-22**

**Title:** **Sister trick again with Abimelech before Isaac is born resulting in a treaty where Abraham owns Beersheba, and then Abraham’s faith is tested with the command to sacrifice his only son.**

**Storyline Summary:**

- **Abraham deceives Abimelech**
- **Abimelech makes a treaty with Abraham**
- **God’s promise to Sarah comes true with Isaac’s birth**
- **Sent Hagar and Ishmael away with nothing**
- **Abraham asked to sacrifice Isaac**
- **God stopped Isaac’s sacrifice**
