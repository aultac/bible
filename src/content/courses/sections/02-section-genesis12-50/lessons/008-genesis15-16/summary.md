

**Summary: Genesis 15-16**

**Title:** **God makes a covenant with Abram, promising to multiply greatly both Isaac and Ishmael, but Hagar runs away and God brings her back.**

**Storyline Summary:**

- **God makes covenant with Abram**
- **Covenant ceremony with the halved animals**
- **Unilateral covenant: only God walks between the animals**
- **God promises Abram a son**
- **God tells Abram his descendants will be slaves**
- **Prophecy of conquest after slavery**
- **Ishmael**
- **Hagar runs away**
- **God prophesies Ishmael’s fate**
- **God brings Hagar back**
