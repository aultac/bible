- Moses’s job here is sort of like trying to explain color to a blind person

Just not part of the story.
- Before you get too proud of your own theory, just remember Job 38:4 where were you when I laid the foundations of the universe?

READ Ch 1 verse 1:

- Genesis 1: God is not within nature, God created nature. Revolutionary. All other creation stories show the god‘s emerging from nature or chaos. Most non-Christians still don’t understand this: ever heard anyone ask who created God?
- ex nihilo (“Barah”): because everything said in Latin sounds important. Used 3 times: heavens and the earth, life, and man. Different from all other creation stories. Moves from chaos to order, not order to chaos through a battle of the gods. First in human history: God is not created. Atheists don’t understand that the Christian God is by definition not created.

READ rest of Ch 1:

- When God said that it was good during creation, the Hebrew word is “tov” which isn’t a question of morally good, and it doesn’t mean perfect. It means well suited For purpose. If God‘s purpose was the ultimate redemption of mankind, and he could have been saying that his creation was well suited to accomplish his goal.
- Let “us“ make man in our image: us? Trinity or Heavenly Host
- Parallelism: ABB prime A prime. Male and female he created them.
- Time was delineated as Holy: Sabbath is a “temple” in time rather than space
- Sabbath teaches you that you are more than your work. We say “I am a farmer”, we define ourselves by our occupation. God says Cain was a worker of the ground and Abel was a keeper of sheep. It is how English works. But that is not what you ARE. You are more than just your work.

**Science (10 mins)**

- STUMBLING BLOCK: You’ll hear more often than any other reason “I don’t believe in the Bible, I believe in ___________ (science)”. But they don’t even know what science is in general, nor what the current scientific consensus even is. This is our brother's stumbling block. We need to know it and have answers for them.
- The believer has to account for the existence of unjust suffering, the atheist has to count for the existence of everything else
- WHAT IS SCIENCE? Repeated experiments. What did in have for lunch 3 Wednesday’s ago? Everything that is not a repeated experiment is just guessing. It’s circumstantial evidence.
- SMUGGLING: the core problem is that we smuggle our trust of repeated experimentation over to apply it to history questions that are only circumstantial. We can't repeat history for the purpose of experimentation.
- REGULAR: Science requires a regular universe. The only reason to believe we have a regular universe is that God created it that way, so we would be able to recognize miracles.
- Modern Science is the Judeo-Christian world views gift to humanity (John Lennox). But the world worshiped the creature rather than the creator. Science only began in Christendom.
- RELATIVITY: universe has properties difficult to explain: flashlight on a train for relativity, E=mc^2 conservation of momentum (mass * velocity), red shift of stars farther away, 10^80 particles and 10^160 possible interactions, expansion of universe is accelerating (but slowing?) - dark matter/energy
- If sitting at the center of the big bang, you would be in the seventh day from the start. And the days get exponentially shorter as the universe expands
- Let there be light: the first thing created is 1: energy (15-7bn). 2: Condensed into rocks and water Milky Way (7-3.5bn), 3: Oceans, Land, Plants (3.5-1.5bn), 4: water and clouds are parted to see Sun Moon Stars (1.5bn-75m), 5: creatures appear in water (750m-250m), 6: land creatures and people (250m-6k)
- Robert Jastrow: “For the scientist who has lived by his faith in the power of reason, the story ends like a bad dream. He has scaled the mountains of ignorance, he is about to conquer the highest peak; as he pulls himself over the final rock, he is greeted by a band of theologians who have been sitting there for centuries.”
