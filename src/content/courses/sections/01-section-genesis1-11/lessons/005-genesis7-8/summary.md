

**Summary: Genesis 7-8**

**Title:** **Noah obeyed, Noah made it through the storm, God remembered Noah.**

**Storyline Summary:**

- **God chose Noah and told him to build the ark**
- **God said what to put in the ark: animals**
- **Noah obeys**
- **Noah walked with God**
- **What God said would happen, happened. (Flood)**
- **God closed the door**
- **Flood subsides and God promises not to destroy the earth with a flood again.**
