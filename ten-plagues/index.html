Exodus 7–12

The Plagues and Egypt’s Gods

A visual companion to the Egyptian Deities page: each plague appears in biblical order, paired with a traditional Egyptian deity or divine-royal claim that the plague is often understood to challenge. Every card keeps both images prominent: the targeted god and an image representing the plague.

Exodus says the Lord would bring judgment “on all the gods of Egypt” (Exodus 12:12), but it does not explicitly map each plague to a named Egyptian deity. The pairings below are common teaching associations, so the page phrases them as interpretive rather than certain.
